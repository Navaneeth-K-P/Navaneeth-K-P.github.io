Corona Monitor

Summer of 2020 •By Navaneeth & Ayesha
Scroll

My first software

This project was part of the I4 challenge conducted by IITD in the summer of 2020. Neither of us had any prior experience in website development. The entire project was completed using self taught concepts.

Through the course of this project, a website based Kiosk was created which enables hospitals to track and monitor Covid-19 patients and their families in accordance with the problem statement given by the organizing committee. The project was completed in a duration of around 24 days.

Languages used : HTML, PHP, MySQL
Team Member : Ayesha Kajol Rafi
